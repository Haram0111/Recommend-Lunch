오늘 뭐먹지

무슬림 추천 음식

소불고기(Beef-Bulgogi)

It is a food that marinate thinly sliced beef in soy sauce and then grill it with svegetables such as onions, mushrooms, and green onions.

닭볶음탕(Dak-bokkeum-tang)

It is a dish made by mixing sliced chicken, potatoes, carrots, and onions together in a spicy sauce made of red pepper powder, putting them in a pot, and then pouring water to boil the soup.

전(Jeon)

It is a food that is grilled in oil with thin coating of flour and egg water on the ingredients. There are various types of Jeon, such as kimchi jeon, green onion jeon, chives jeon, zucchini jeon, and mushroom jeon.

해물된장찌개(Haemuldoenjangjjigae)

It is a stew boiled with doenjang(soybean paste) in broth made of anchovies and seafood such as squid, clams, shrimp, and blue crabs, and vegetables such as zucchini and onions.

생선구이(Saengseon gui)

It is a food that is eaten by grilling various fish using an oven or a pan. They grill and eat fish such as mackerel, saury, and hairtail, and it ranges from seasoned with salt to seasoned with soy sauce or red pepper paste.

매운탕(maeuntang)

It is a boiling food made by shellfish, onions, water parsley, radish, green onions, and peppers made with red pepper powder using fish as the main ingredient. It mainly uses cod, rockfish, and pollack.

삼계탕(Samgyetang)

It is a food that is boiled for a long time after putting sticky rice, garlic, jujube, ginseng, and herbal medicine in the stomach of a chicken and then pouring water.

닭칼국수(Dakkalguksu)

It is a food that is boiled with kalguksu(noodles) in the broth made by boiling chicken, and then spread the chicken flesh on top.

해물순두부찌개(Haemulsundubujjigae)

It is a food boiled with vegetables and spicy seasoning using seafood such as squid, shellfish, and shrimp and soft tofu as the main ingredients. Sometimes eat it with eggs.

수제비(Sujebi)

It is a food made by tearing flour kneaded in broth made with anchovies, kelp, and radish in an appropriate size. It usually contains potatoes and zucchini.
